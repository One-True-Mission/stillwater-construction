Our Story

Built on Faith. Backed by 10 Years of Experience.

Stillwater Construction & Roofing is family-owned and operated by Alex and Meriya Poole. With 10 years of experience in commercial and residential construction management, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality construction services across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, from interior finish outs and remodels to concrete, electrical, and roofing install and restoration.

Built on strong Christian values, our company is guided by faith, integrity, honesty, and doing the right thing, on and off the jobsite. Our faith shapes how we conduct business, treat our clients, and stand behind our work.

We believe in doing things the right way, from the first call to the final walk through. That means clear communication, dependable workmanship, projects completed on time, and every client treated with respect, accountability, and care. That's the standard. We don't deviate from it.

"He leads me beside still waters. He restores my soul." Psalm 23, the verse behind the Stillwater name.
