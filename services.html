What We Do

The Full Build. Done Right.

Real construction work, finished properly. Every job carried from the first call to the final walk through by Stillwater Construction & Roofing.

1 Top of the House

Roofing: Install & Restoration

Commercial and residential roofing, install and restoration. New roofs, full replacements, and restoration work that brings a storm-worn roof back to where it should be. Quality materials, clean installs, and a job site left spotless.

Commercial & Residential
2 Full Builds

Interior Finish Out & Remodeling

Demolition, framing in metal and wood, insulation, wall finishes, tile, dropped ceilings, HVAC, awnings, and restroom accessories. From a bare shell to a finished space, one company handles every step of the work.

3 Groundwork

Concrete & Flatwork

Patio and driveway extensions, foundations, flatwork, and grading and dirt work. Concrete poured and finished by hand with proper reinforcement, graded right so water goes where it should and the slab holds up through Texas weather.

4 Specialty

Electrical Work

A full electrical scope of work: undergrounds, wall rough in, ceiling rough in, light fixtures, light poles, and MDPs and switch gear. From residential lighting to commercial distribution, wired clean and done to spec.

Full Commercial Scope
5 Specialty

Interior & Exterior Painting

Painting done the right way, inside and out. Proper prep, clean lines, even coats, and the right paint for the job. From a single accent wall to a full exterior repaint, we finish what we start with no half-painted handoffs.

6 Craft Work

Custom Cabinetry & Finish Work

Kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities, entertainment built-ins, mantles, crown molding, wainscoting, and trim work. The finish details that turn a job from done to done right, all built and installed by our team.

7 Outdoor

Fencing, Patios & Outdoor Builds

Fences, patios, decks, awnings, walkways, and outdoor structures. Built square, sealed, and set to hold up through North Texas heat, wind, and hail. Paired with our concrete work for complete outdoor projects.

8 Ground-Up Builds

New Home Builds

Ground-up builds for clients who want something other than a tract builder. Ten years of construction management experience means the schedule, the trades, and the budget are run right, from foundation to finishes.

9 Rough-In to Fixtures

Plumbing

Plumbing handled as part of your remodel, finish out, or build. Bathroom and kitchen reroutes, fixture installs, and repairs, coordinated under one contract so you're not chasing a separate contractor to keep your job moving.

10 Mechanical

HVAC

AC installs, replacements, ductwork, and mechanical work. Available as part of finish outs, remodels, and new builds, or as standalone jobs. One contract through us, one team coordinating the work, one finished result.

Also Available

Commercial Projects

Ten years of commercial construction management behind every job. Tenant finish outs, commercial roofing, electrical distribution, and light commercial construction, with the same single-point-of-contact approach as our residential work.
