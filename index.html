Who We Are

Family-Owned. Faith-Guided. Built on Doing It Right.

Stillwater Construction & Roofing is a family-owned and operated construction company serving homeowners and businesses across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. With 10 years of experience in commercial and residential construction management, we take on everything from interior finish outs and full remodels to concrete, electrical, and roofing install and restoration.

We believe in doing things the right way, from the first call to the final walk through. That means dependable workmanship, clear communication, and projects completed on time, with every client treated with respect, accountability, and care.

Guided by faith, integrity, and honesty, on and off the jobsite. Psalm 23:1-3.

What We Build

One Company. Dirt to Finishes.

Roofing, finish outs, concrete, electrical, painting, cabinetry, and more. Carried from start to finish by Stillwater Construction & Roofing. Here's a few of the things we do.

Roofing: Install & Restoration

Commercial and residential roofing. New installs, full replacements, and restoration work that brings a storm-worn roof back to where it should be.

Interior Finish Out & Remodeling

Demolition, framing in metal and wood, insulation, wall finishes, tile, dropped ceilings, and restroom accessories. The full interior, finished clean.

Concrete & Flatwork

Patio and driveway extensions, foundations, flatwork, and grading and dirt work. Poured, finished, and set to hold up through Texas weather.

Electrical Work

Full electrical scope. Undergrounds, wall and ceiling rough in, light fixtures, light poles, and MDPs and switch gear for commercial jobs.

Interior & Exterior Painting

Painting done the right way, inside and out. Proper prep, clean lines, even coats, and the right paint for the job. From a wall to a whole building.

Custom Cabinetry & Finish Work

Kitchen cabinets, vanities, built-ins, mantles, crown molding, trim. The finish details that turn a job from done to done right.
