5. Licensing in Texas

Texas does not require or issue a state-level general contractor license for residential construction. This is a fact about Texas law, not a deficiency of any contractor. Many property owners aren't aware of this when they ask about contractor licensing.

Where state law or local jurisdictions (city or county) require permits, registrations, licensed trades, or specialty credentials for specific work, those requirements are met before the work starts. Documentation for a specific project is available on request during your estimate.
